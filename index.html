Understanding Commercial Papers (CPs)

A commercial paper (CP) is an unsecured, short-term debt security issued by corporations to fund immediate operational needs like accounts payable, inventory, or short-term obligations. In Nigeria, CPs are overseen by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) and act as a critical financing mechanism for businesses. They also offer investors attractive returns compared to conventional savings accounts or government-issued treasury bills.
