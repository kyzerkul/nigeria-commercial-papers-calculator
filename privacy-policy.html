Introduction

Welcome to the Nigeria Commercial Paper Calculator. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you provide while using our calculator.

Information We Collect

Our calculator operates entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or calculation inputs. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

Usage Data

We may use basic analytics tools to understand how users interact with our calculator. This information is anonymous and helps us improve our service. The data collected may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Usage patterns
  • Geographic location (country level only)

Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Any local storage used is strictly for improving calculator functionality.

Third-Party Services

Our calculator does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect user data.

Data Security

Since all calculations are performed locally, your financial data never leaves your device. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect our website from unauthorized access.

Changes to Privacy Policy
